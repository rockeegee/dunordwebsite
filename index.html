FAQ

Common questions.

How long does it take to get my site live? +
Most sites go live within 3–5 business days of receiving your completed intake form. If revisions are needed, we handle those quickly too.
Why is DuNord so much cheaper than other agencies? +
Most agencies charge for complexity you don’t need — custom animations, e-commerce systems, blogging platforms, and ongoing strategy calls. For local service businesses, the fundamentals drive the highest ROI: a clean design, your services and pricing, a phone number, and a professional domain. We’ve refined our process around delivering exactly those fundamentals efficiently, at a price that makes sense for a small business.
Do I need to provide anything? +
Just fill out our short intake form — business name, services, pricing, logo (if you have one), and any photos. Takes about 10 minutes.
What if I want changes after launch? +
Small text and content updates are included in the $99/month plan. Larger changes like a redesign or structural updates will be quoted at an hourly rate.
Can I cancel the monthly plan? +
Yes — no long-term contracts, cancel any time. Your custom domain stays yours. Site files are not transferable upon cancellation, but you’re always welcome to restart the plan.
Do I need a custom domain? +
Not required, but strongly recommended. A domain like YourBusiness.com looks far more professional and helps customers find you on Google. One-time $100 fee — we handle all setup.
