FAQ

Common questions.

How long does it take to get my site live? +
Most sites go live within 3–5 business days of receiving your completed intake form. If revisions are needed, we handle those quickly too.
Why is DuNord so much cheaper than other agencies? +
Most agencies charge for complexity you don't need — custom animations, e-commerce systems, blogging platforms, and ongoing strategy calls. Research consistently shows that for local service businesses, the fundamentals drive the highest ROI: a clean design, your services and pricing, a phone number, and a professional domain. Those are the things that make a customer trust you and pick up the phone. We've refined our entire process around delivering exactly those fundamentals — efficiently, and at a price that actually makes sense for a small business.
Do I need to provide anything? +
Just fill out our short intake form — it covers your business name, services, pricing, logo (if you have one), and any photos. That's it. Takes about 10 minutes.
What if I want changes after launch? +
Small text and content updates are included in the $99/month plan — things like updating your hours, adding a service, or tweaking pricing. Larger changes such as a redesign or significant structural updates will be quoted at an hourly rate.
Can I cancel the monthly plan? +
Yes — no long-term contracts, cancel any time. If you purchased a custom domain, that stays yours. Please note that the website itself is built and maintained by DuNord Digital — site files are not transferable upon cancellation. If you'd like to continue having a website after cancelling, you're always welcome to restart the plan.
Do I need a custom domain? +
Not required, but strongly recommended. A custom domain like YourBusiness.com looks far more professional and helps customers find you through Google. It's a one-time $100 fee — we handle all the setup.
